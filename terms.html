2. What KinBeat does

KinBeat is a mobile app that helps families watch over an elderly loved one. It looks at gentle signals from the phone (unlocks, volume presses, step count) to understand if the person is using their phone normally. If the phone has been still for a long time, KinBeat asks the person if they are okay. If they do not reply, a family member is notified.

What KinBeat does not do

  • It is not a medical device. It does not diagnose, monitor, or treat any medical condition.
  • It is not a substitute for emergency services. The "I need help" button tries to dial the emergency number, but connection cannot be guaranteed.
  • It is not a substitute for proper care. It does not replace a caregiver, family member, or professional support.
  • It cannot protect against every situation. Phones can run out of battery, lose network, be left behind, or have permissions revoked. KinBeat cannot alert anyone in those cases.

You must not rely on KinBeat alone for the safety of any person.
